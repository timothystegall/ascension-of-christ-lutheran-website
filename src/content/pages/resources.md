        <div class="text-left pt-[25px]">
            <p class="text-white mb-3">
                This curated list serves as a digital library for those seeking to learn more about the <i>"faith once delivered to the saints,"</i> offering access to Lutheran doctrine and our rich liturgical heritage. From the 1580 Book of Concord to the timeless treasury of our hymnody, these resources are listed here to offer nourishment for your faith, and clarify what we believe, teach, and confess.
            </p>
            <h3 class="text-light font-bold mb-2">Links:</h3>
            <ul class="text-white mb-3">
                <li class="mb-2"><a href="https://www.lcms.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">LCMS Website</a> - AoC is a member congregation of The Lutheran Church - Missouri Synod.</li>
                <li class="mb-2"><a href="https://www.englishdistrict.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">English District</a> - AoC holds membership in the English District of the LCMS.</li>
                <li class="mb-2"><a href="https://bookofconcord.cph.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">The 1580 Book of Concord</a> - Our Lutheran Confessions are publicly available to read for free at this website.</li>
                <li class="mb-2"><a href="https://issuesetc.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Issues Etc</a> - A weekly Confessional Lutheran radio talk show and podcast. The program features expert guests, expansive topics, while always extolling Christ. It is hosted by LCMS Pastor Todd Wilken, and layman Jeff Schwartz. AoC is a supporting congregation of Issues!</li>
                <li class="mb-2"><a href="https://www.kfuo.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">KFUO Radio</a> - The online radio station of the Lutheran Church - Missouri Synod. Visit their website to listen to excellent podcasts and music. Find daily devotions, Bible studies, recorded worship services, and more!</li>
                <li class="mb-2"><a href="https://www.cph.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Concordia Publishing House</a> - The publishing arm of the LCMS. It exists for the purposes of strengthening and aiding members in their proclamation of the Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ. CPH provides great resources to strengthen you and your family in the Christian faith.</li>
                <li class="mb-2"><a href="https://www.youtube.com/@BryanWolfmueller" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Pastor Bryan Wolfmueller</a> - The YouTube Channel of LCMS Pastor Bryan Wolfmueller. Pastor Wolfmueller is an excellent, knowledgeable teacher of the Christian faith and Lutheran doctrine.</li>
                <li class="mb-2"><a href="https://www.youtube.com/@JordanCooper" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Dr. Jordan B. Cooper</a> - The YouTube Channel of AALC (the American Assocation of Lutheran Churches, whom the LCMS is in fellowship with) Pastor Dr. Jordan Cooper. Dr. Cooper provides in-depth teaching on doctrine, church history, apologetics, and more. He also runs an independent publishing house for Lutherans called <a href="https://www.justandsinner.org/">Just & Sinner</a>.</li>
                <li class="mb-2"><a href="https://www.youtube.com/@ScholasticLutherans" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Scholastic Lutherans</a> - A YouTube Channel ran by a group of LCMS laymen. They offer excellent content regarding Lutheran Scholasticism, theology, and philosophy: including material from prominent historic Lutheran theologians such as Martin Chemnitz, Johann Gerhard, and more.</li>
                <li class="mb-2"><a href="http://kretzmannproject.org/">The Kretzmann Project</a> - An online edition of sainted Lutheran theologian Paul E. Kretzmann's excellent <i>Popular Commentary of the Bible</i>, completed in 1924, which is now in the public domain.</li>
            </ul>
